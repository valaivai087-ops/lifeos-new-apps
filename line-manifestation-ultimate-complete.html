✍️ 英文寫作16大高分句型
① 情感來襲句型 — A rush/flood/wave/surge of +
中文：一股/一陣情緒襲來
表示情緒突然、強烈出現
English:
A flush/flood/wave/ripple/surge of + emotion
sweep over / flood over / come over / pass through + sb
📌例句：
A rush of joy flooded her heart.
A wave of sadness overwhelmed him.
A surge of excitement swept over her.
② 被情緒淹沒 — Be overwhelmed with +
Sb + be overwhelmed/flooded/consumed/engulfed + emotion
She was overwhelmed with sorrow → 她悲傷到極點
③ 如此…以至於… — So…that felt as if +
Sb was so…that he/she felt as if…
He was so happy that he felt as if in heaven
④ 情緒蔓延（擬人） — Creep/crawl on +
Emotion + creep/crawl on/upon sb
Fear crawled on him → 恐懼蔓延
⑤ 情緒戰勝人 — Get the better of +
Emotion + get the better/best of + sb
Excitement got the better of me → 我太激動了
⑥ 環境烘托情感 — Absolute Phrase +
The breeze blowing softly, happiness crept up
The rain pattering softly, tears flowed freely
⑦ 說話動詞升級 — Speak Verbs Variety +
分類動詞含義
小聲whisper, mutter低聲細語
大聲shout, scream, exclaim尖叫、驚呼
問答ask, reply, respond詢問、回答
狀態stammer, gasp, sigh, sob結巴、喘息、嘆氣、嗚咽
⑧ 六大動作系統 — Six Key Actions +
看： stare at(盯)、glance at(瞥)、scan(掃視)、fix on(聚焦)
走： sneak(偷走)、tiptoe(踮腳)、wander(閒逛)、stride(大步)
跑： rush/dash(衝)、flee(逃)、sprint(衝刺)、bolt(突然跑)
笑/哭： grin/beam(笑)、weep/sob(哭)
⑨ 動作鏈 — Action Chain (A, B, and C) +
❌ 弱：He walked. He hugged her. He kissed her.
✅ 強：He walked towards her, hugged her tightly, and kissed her passionately.
⑩ 非謂語句型 — Participle Phrases (V-ing/V-ed) +
V-ing: Checking her phone, she was nervous.
V-ed: Stunned by the scene, she stood still.
⑪ 獨立主格（超高級） — Absolute Phrase +
Eyes brimming with tears, she hugged her mom
Heart racing, he opened the letter
常用：Eyes brimming 眼含淚 | Heart racing 心狂跳 | Face flushing 臉發紅
⑫ 形容詞句型 — Adjective Phrase +
Depressed and helpless, she cried → 因為很沮喪，她哭了
Confused and scared, he ran away
⑬ 進階句型 — Occurrence & Emphasis +
It occurred/hit/dawned on sb that…
It was at that moment that I realized…
It was love that makes life meaningful
⑭ 倒裝句（高分神器） — Inversion +
So angry was he that he couldn't speak
Not until she left did he realize his mistake
Had I known, I would never have done it
⑮ 修辭與無靈主語 — Rhetoric & Inanimate Subject +
明喻：She was as graceful as a swan
擬人：The breeze kissed her face
誇張：My blood froze
無靈主語：The past years saw… / Shanghai witnessed…
⑯ 萬能結尾 — Unforgettable Endings +
Many things faded from memory, but this stayed clear
Had it not been for this experience, I would never have…
Looking back, I realize how precious that moment was.

💎 高維金句·全集（143+條）
【核心升維模式】
升維本質：「不是追求結果，而是建立能力系統」
❌ 低維：追求「結果」（收入、成績、快樂）
✅ 高維：建立「能力系統」（創造價值的能力、理解能力、幸福能力）
【第一輪·基礎升維（20項）】
不是收入，而是價值創造能力
Not income, but your ability to create value
收入是結果，能力才是根本。掌握創造價值的系統，收入自然增長。
不是成績，而是理解能力
Not grades, but your ability to understand
成績是表面指標。理解的深度才是真正競爭力。
不是流量，而是信任品質
Not traffic, but the quality of trust
單純流量無法變現。高品質的信任才是長期價值。
不是努力，而是系統品質
Not hard work, but the quality of your system
再努力的低效系統也無法成功。優化系統最關鍵。
不是舒服，而是更新能力
Not comfort, but your ability to update yourself
舒服意味著停滯。學會持續更新才能應對變化。
不是控制別人，而是管理自己
Not controlling others, but managing yourself
你無法控制別人。真正的力量來自自我掌控。
不是追求完美，而是持續改善
Not perfection, but continuous improvement
完美主義會導致癱瘓。1%的日進步365天後變37倍。
不是忙碌，而是有效產出
Not being busy, but producing meaningful results
忙碌感很容易迷惑人。真正的成功在於產出品質。
不是知道很多，而是做到很多
Not knowing more, but doing more
知識本身沒有價值。只有轉化為行動的知識才有價值。
不是資訊量，而是判斷品質
Not information volume, but judgment quality
資訊時代的困境是過量。真正的稀缺是做正確判斷的能力。
不是記憶力，而是思考能力
Not memory, but thinking ability
記憶力可被機器替代。思考能力是AI無法複製的。
不是速度，而是方向
Not speed, but direction
向錯誤的方向跑得再快也會失敗。正確的方向才是決定成敗的因素。
不是時間管理，而是能量管理
Not time management, but energy management
時間對每個人都是24小時。真正的差別在能量利用效率。
不是情緒壓抑，而是情緒調節能力
Not suppressing emotions, but regulating them
壓抑情緒會導致爆發。學會調節情緒才能掌控人生。
不是避免失敗，而是快速修正能力
Not avoiding failure, but correcting quickly
失敗是必然的。重要的是失敗後能多快恢復。
不是答案，而是提問能力
Not answers, but the ability to ask questions
給答案不如教提問。好問題比好答案更值錢。
不是抱怨環境，而是設計環境
Not complaining, but designing the environment
抱怨改變不了現實。環境設計能改變行為模式。
不是改變世界，而是提升自己
Not changing the world, but improving yourself
改變世界太遠大。提升自己是唯一能掌控的。
不是期待機會，而是創造機會
Not waiting for opportunities, but creating them
機會不會主動出現。創造機會需要準備+行動。
不是一直消費，而是持續創造
Not consumption, but continuous creation
消費只會消耗資源。創造才能積累資源。
